### Business Engagement

#### Meeting Standards
The team follows GitLab meeting practices and standards.

- Every meeting has an agenda.
- Notes are added to the agenda inline with topics and questions.
- All agendas are stored in the BSA Meeting Notes [shared drive](https://drive.google.com/drive/folders/1maD_xGpiIgd_ZENN5pxj110S-xZ6hjjk).

#### Recurring Meetings

**Weekly Lead-to-Cash BSAs**<br>

Every Monday at 10:30am PST the BSAs get together to go through the projects that are being worked on accross the team. During this meeting we use [Rolly](https://gitlab.com/gitlab-com/business-technology/business-technology-ops/-/issues?scope=all&utf8=%E2%9C%93&state=all&label_name%5B%5D=EntApps-weekly-rollup) to cover every current project. 

> The time of this meeting might change according to the BSA's calendars.

**Weekly Enterprise Applications Sync**<br>

Every week the Enterprise Apps team has a dedicated hour to sync up. Hosted by the Senior Director of Enterprise Applications, the team discusses current urgent and important activities that impacts the whole team.

> The time of this meeting varies every week to accommodate the different timezones of the Enterprise Apps team members.

**Finance Engagement Meeting**<br>

Every 2 weeks on Wednesdays at 8:30am PST the BSAs (together with the Business Technology leaders) meet with the Finance key stakeholders to update the business with the latests projects. 
During this meeting we use [Rolly](https://gitlab.com/gitlab-com/business-technology/business-technology-ops/-/issues?scope=all&utf8=%E2%9C%93&state=all&label_name%5B%5D=EntApps-weekly-rollup) to cover project status, what's in progress and what is blocked. 

#### No-Meetings Fridays
While we can't promise we'll never have meetings on Fridays, the team has adopted [No Meetings Fridays](/handbook/communication/#no-meetings-fridays).
